<Section icon={Target} title="Optimized Farming Routes">

Efficient farming requires a systematic approach. Instead of digging straight down, focus on clearing horizontal pockets in layers where specific gems and ores are concentrated.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation">
    Visit the surface lobby to clear your inventory, repair gear, and purchase necessary explosives from the shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Layer Selection">
    Choose a layer that matches your current pickaxe power. Farming in layers too deep will result in slow block-breaking and wasted time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Clearing">
    Use sticky explosives to clear dense ore pockets quickly. Save your basic pickaxe durability for high-value rare ore veins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Return and Process">
    Return to the surface once your backpack is full. Smelt your raw ores into ingots before selling them to maximize your gold profit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
