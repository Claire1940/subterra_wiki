<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Subterra Golden Fragment Farming Route">

Follow this optimized route to safely navigate the deep biomes and secure your fragments with minimal risk.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Loadout">
    Equip your highest-durability pickaxe and pack plenty of healing consumables. Clear your inventory of non-essential items to maximize your carrying capacity for ores and shards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Shipwreck Biome">
    Descend through the main cavern shaft until you reach the transition zone marked by wooden planks and pirate debris. This indicates you have entered the Shipwreck territory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Cold Ice Lake">
    Locate the narrow crevice near the ship's hull. Press the prone key to crawl through the gap. This path leads directly to the flat, frozen expanse of the Cold Ice Lake.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Frost Guardian">
    Trigger the boss encounter on the cold ice. Use ranged attacks to stay safe from its ground-slam ability. Defeating the boss yields an Ice Key and a chance at raw fragments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock the Golden Vaults">
    Use your newly acquired Ice Key to unlock the sealed gate at the back of the frozen cavern. Inside, you will find rich veins containing the coveted golden fragments.
  </Step>
</Steps>
