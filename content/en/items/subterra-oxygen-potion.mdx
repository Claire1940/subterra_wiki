<Section icon={Wind} title="Oxygen Mechanics and Survival Basics">

In the procedurally generated depths of Subterra, oxygen is just as valuable as the rarest Diamond. As you descend past the Dirt and Stone layers, the environment becomes increasingly oppressive. Managing your **subterra oxygen potion** supply and understanding how depletion works is the difference between a profitable haul and losing your inventory to a "Return by Death" scenario. The oxygen system dictates the duration of your mining loop, forcing a tactical balance between greed for ores and the necessity of returning to the surface.

<Table>
| Mechanic | Effect on Survival | Best Countermeasure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oxygen Depletion** | Gradually drains while underground | **subterra oxygen potion** / Return Potions |
| **Deep Layer Pressure** | Faster drain in Darkstone and Schwarzfrost | **Extra Oxygen** Ability Card (Level 10) |
| **Monster Damage** | Reduces health, making oxygen panic worse | **Small Health Potions** / Parry (F Key) |
| **Environmental Hazards** | TNT and falling rocks can trap you | **Sticky C4** for quick emergency exits |
</Table>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Running out of oxygen doesn't just slow you down; it leads to a rapid health drain. If you are deep in the Permafrost layer without a **subterra oxygen potion** or a Wormhole Potion, your chances of reaching the surface lobby alive are significantly reduced.
</Callout>

Oxygen management is further influenced by your movement speed and mining efficiency. Players who invest in **Walk Speed** and **Lighter Pickaxe** cards find that they can cover more ground and mine more blocks before their air supply runs out. This efficiency effectively "increases" your oxygen by reducing the time spent on each task.

</Section>

<Section icon={FlaskConical} title="subterra oxygen potion: Crafting and Consumables">

Crafting in Subterra is handled at the **Crafting Altar** and the **Smeltery** located in the Overworld Plaza. To prepare for the deeper layers, you must gather specific materials from both monsters and the environment. While the **subterra oxygen potion** and health recovery items are the primary focus, you must also understand the prerequisite materials like Water Flasks and Life Shards.

<Table>
| Item Name | Category | Primary Recipe Ingredients | Main Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Small Health Potion** | Potion | 1x Water Flask, 2x Roots, 1x Life Shard | Restores 25 HP immediately |
| **subterra oxygen potion** | Utility | Water Flask, Shards, Layer-specific flora | Refills oxygen meter for deep runs |
| **Water Flask** | Material | Dropped by Monsters / Found in Chests | Base ingredient for all liquid potions |
| **Life Shard** | Material | Dropped by Slimes and Zombies | Required for health-based consumables |
| **Roots** | Material | Gathered from Dirt and Stone layers | Common filler for basic utility recipes |
</Table>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Crafting Strategy">
Don't sell your **Slime Chunks** or **Life Shards** early on. While they offer a small amount of Gold, their value in crafting **Small Health Potions** and **Sticky Explosives** is much higher when you are attempting to break into the Darkstone layer (Y -435).
</Callout>

To unlock higher-tier recipes, you must find **Recipe Scrolls** in chests or as progression rewards. The **Basic Utility Item Recipe Scroll** is particularly important as it unlocks the ability to craft keys and survival potions. Without these, you are reliant on shop prices, which can quickly drain your Gold reserves during the early-to-mid game progression.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Deep Layer Survival">

Surviving the transition from the Stone layer to the Darkstone and Schwarzfrost layers requires a disciplined approach. Use the following steps to ensure you don't waste your **subterra oxygen potion** or lose your hard-earned materials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Lobby Preparation">
    Before descending, visit the Shop to buy **Small Bombs** and ensure your **Water Flask** supply is replenished. Check the **Codes NPC** for any active rewards like "100KMEMBERS" which provides free potions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Descent and Buffing">
    Move through the Dirt and Stone layers quickly. Do not stop to mine common Rock unless you need it for a specific upgrade. Once you hit Y -400, consume a **subterra oxygen potion** if your meter is below 50%.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Targeting Rare Ores">
    In the Permafrost layer, look for **Moonstone** and **Azurite**. These are your high-value targets. Use **Sticky Dynamite** to clear clusters quickly, minimizing the time spent in high-drain environments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Emergency Extraction">
    If your oxygen reaches 10% or your health drops below 30%, use a **Return by Death Potion** or find the nearest **Wormhole**. It is always better to lose a few minutes of travel time than to lose your entire inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Note">
The **F key** (Block/Parry) is your best friend when fighting **Darkstone Skeletons**. A successful parry prevents health loss, which in turn prevents the panic that often leads players to forget their oxygen levels.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Equipment Upgrades and Resource Management">

Your equipment serves as the foundation for your survival. While the **subterra oxygen potion** provides temporary relief, a Tier 10 Backpack allows you to carry more resources, making each trip more profitable. Similarly, a high-tier Pickaxe reduces the "time-to-mine," which is the most significant factor in oxygen consumption.

<Callout type="tip" title="Inventory Management">
When your backpack is nearly full, discard low-value items like **Dirt** or **Rock** to make room for **Raw Gold** or **Diamonds**. This ensures that every second of oxygen spent underground translates into maximum Gold gain.
</Callout>

Upgrading your backpack to Tier 10 is a major milestone. It requires a massive variety of gems, including **Ruby**, **Emerald**, and **Diamond**. Saving these gems specifically for the Tier 10 upgrade—rather than selling them for quick cash—is the mark of an experienced Subterra player. The extra 75 slots provided by this tier allow for massive mining sessions that justify the use of multiple **subterra oxygen potion** consumables.
