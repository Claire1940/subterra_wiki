### How the Gifting Mechanic Works

To gift an item to another player, you must interact directly with them in the game world. The system relies on physical proximity and active item holding. Follow these precise steps to gift items safely:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Item to Your Hotbar">
    Open your inventory by pressing **E**. Drag the item you want to gift (such as a potion, weapon, or key) into an active hotbar slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hold the Item in Your Hand">
    Select the hotbar slot so your character is physically holding the item in their hand in the game world.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Approach Your Friend">
    Walk up to the player you want to gift the item to until you are standing directly next to them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Hold the Gift Key">
    Press and hold the **G key** for **1 second**. A prompt will appear, and holding it down completes the transfer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Accidental Gift Protection">
The developers updated the gifting key from the default interaction key to **G** and increased the hold duration to **1 second** to prevent players from accidentally giving away precious gear during hectic fights.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Gifting Requirements and Tier Restrictions">

To maintain game balance and prevent high-level players from completely breaking the progression of beginners, Subterra enforces strict level and tier restrictions on gifted items. You cannot simply drop end-game gear to a brand-new player who has just started the game.

### The 20-Tier Rule
The primary restriction revolves around the recipient's pickaxe tier. An item can only be successfully gifted if its item tier is **within 20 tiers** of the recipient's current pickaxe tier.

| Recipient Pickaxe Tier | Maximum Giftable Item Tier | Minimum Giftable Item Tier | Gifting Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 (Starter)** | Tier 21 | Tier 1 | Allowed |
| **Tier 10** | Tier 30 | Tier 1 | Allowed |
| **Tier 25** | Tier 45 | Tier 5 | Allowed |
| **Tier 50** | Tier 70 | Tier 30 | Allowed |

### Giftable vs. Non-Giftable Items
Not every item in Subterra can be transferred between players. Refer to the table below to see what you can share:

| Item Category | Giftable Status | Notes & Conditions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Consumables** | **Yes** | Potions, Keys, and Recipe Scrolls must be held to gift. |
| **Weapons** | **Yes** | Swords and Bows are giftable if within the 20-tier limit. |
| **Shields** | **Yes** | Must be equipped on your hotbar and held in hand to transfer. |
| **Explosives** | **Yes** | Bombs, Dynamite, and C4 can be gifted freely. |
| **Chrono Shards** | **No** | Shards are bound to your account and cannot be gifted directly. |
