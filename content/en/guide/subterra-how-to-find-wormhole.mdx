Wormholes do not spawn on the Overworld surface hub. You must descend past the starter Dirt Layer to begin finding them. They are primarily found in the following three environments:

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How Wormhole Pairing and Teleportation Work">

Understanding the physics of a wormhole will prevent you from getting disoriented or trapped deep underground. In **subterra**, wormholes operate under strict pairing rules that make them highly reliable once discovered.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Paired Generation">
    Wormholes always spawn in **identical pairs**. When a wormhole structure is generated, a matching exit wormhole is created somewhere else in the same layer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Persistent Coordinates">
    Once a pair is generated, entering the first wormhole guarantees you will land at the **exact same predetermined coordinate** every time you jump through it again.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Two-Way Travel">
    Wormholes are fully **bi-directional**. You can jump back through the destination wormhole to return instantly to your original starting point.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Because of this pairing mechanism, you can safely use a discovered wormhole as a temporary highway. If you find a rich vein of Gold, Platinum, or Diamond near a destination wormhole, you can mine the area, jump back through to your starting point, and easily secure a safe path back to the surface.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware of Different Pairs">
Entering a different, unrelated wormhole in the same layer will link to a completely different randomized location. Do not assume all wormholes lead to the same hub!
</Callout>
