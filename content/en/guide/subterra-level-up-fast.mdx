<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrade Order That Keeps XP Flowing">

The fastest leveling route is built on upgrade timing. You want more mining power, then more carry space, then better efficiency cards. That order keeps your runs productive and stops you from wasting materials on side systems too early.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spend Smarter">
Do not burn rare gems or late-game materials on side crafts before your core upgrades feel stable. A stronger loop beats a flashy one.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Finish the tutorial and grab your first rewards">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade your pickaxe first">

  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade your backpack when runs end too soon">

  <Step num="4" title="Add efficiency cards after your core gear is stable">

<Section icon={Target} title="Best Layers and XP Targets">

Deep layers are not automatically better. You level faster when the layer matches your gear. In **Subterra level up fast** planning, the early goal is stable ore gain, then safe monster XP, then deeper material routes once your cards and backpack catch up.

<Callout type="info" title="Layer Rule">
Move down when your current layer feels easy, profitable, and predictable. If fights are slowing you down, stay shallow longer.
</Callout>

| Layer | Best Value | Risk Level | When to Move On |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dirt Layer | Coal, Copper, Tin | Low | When surface ores stop funding upgrades |
| Stone Layer | Iron, Silver, Gold, gems | Medium | When you can clear fights and keep your bag full |
| Darkstone Layer | Cobalt, Nocturnite, deeper structures | High | When your weapon and mobility are reliable |
| Permafrost Layer | Titanium, Wolframite, crystals | Very High | When survival tools and recovery are ready |
| Schwarzfrost Layer | Endgame resources and boss routes | Extreme | When you can handle long hostile runs |

| Enemy | Spawn Area | EXP | Why Farm It |
|---|---|---|---|
| Slime | Dirt, Stone | 35 EXP | Early safe combat and sticky materials |
| Zombie | Dirt, Stone | 35 EXP | Simple early XP while mining routes stay open |
| Bomb Skeleton | Stone | 35 EXP | Good XP if you keep distance and avoid panic |
| Darkstone Skeleton | Darkstone, Permafrost | 35 EXP | Better for prepared players with stronger gear |
| Ice Elemental | Permafrost | 2000 EXP | High reward, but only when your build can survive the fight |

The main rule is simple: farm the layer where you can keep moving. A perfect deep route that forces you to retreat every minute is worse than a reliable mid-layer circuit that keeps the ore and XP rolling.

<Callout type="warning" title="Do Not Rush Permafrost">
Permafrost and Schwarzfrost are strong progression zones, but they punish weak gear. Enter them only when your mining speed, combat tools, and recovery items all feel ready.
</Callout>

</Section>
