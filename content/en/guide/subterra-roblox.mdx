<Section icon={Target} title="First Run Plan and Progression">

  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade One Core Stat">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Layers, Enemies, and Combat Habits">

Depth changes the game fast. Shallow zones are about building momentum, while deeper areas punish sloppy positioning and empty recovery. You do not need to fight everything. In fact, the best miners often avoid fights that do not improve loot, access, or quest progress.

| Layer Stage | Typical Risk | Smart Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Surface and hub | Low | Prepare, sell, and reset |
| Early cave routes | Moderate | Learn movement and mining rhythm |
| Mid-depth routes | High | Bring a plan and a backup exit |
| Deep routes | Very high | Only enter with a purpose |
| Boss-adjacent zones | Extreme | Prioritize survival over greed |

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Mindset">
Treat combat as route protection, not as your main objective. Winning a fight is good; keeping a profitable run alive is better.
</Callout>
