<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrade Priority: Pickaxe, Backpack, and Materials">

Your first major decision is simple: make mining faster before you try to make every run longer. A stronger pickaxe clears space, while a bigger backpack turns that speed into profit.

| Stage | Spend On | Save These Materials | Main Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter phase | Pickaxe tiers 2-4 | Rock, Gold, Coal, Raw Copper | Break blocks faster and reach stable ore loops |
| Inventory bottleneck | Backpack tiers 2-3 | Coal, Raw Copper, Raw Tin | Reduce surface trips and keep more ore per run |
| Midgame push | Pickaxe tiers 5-8 | Tin Ingot, Iron, Gold | Keep pace with deeper layers and harder blocks |
| Gem phase | Backpack and recipe planning | Citrine, Topaz, Amethyst, Emerald | Avoid wasting rare materials on low-value spend |

The safest material rule is to hoard gem items when they appear in upgrade recipes later. The source data shows gems like Citrine, Topaz, Amethyst, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, and Diamond being used in progression costs, so random spending can slow your climb.

<Callout type="success" title="Best Upgrade Order">
Pickaxe first, backpack second, then card support. That order keeps your runs efficient without forcing you to overcommit rare resources too early.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Layers, Ores, and Structure Targets">

Subterra’s depth curve is what gives the game its long tail. Each layer changes the ore pool, enemy pressure, and the value of staying underground a little longer.

| Layer | Typical Depth | Key Ores and Resources | Main Threat | Best Goal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dirt Layer | Surface to around Y -35 | Coal, Copper, Tin, Roots | Slimes, Zombies | Build your first material stockpile |
| Stone Layer | Starts around Y -35 | Iron, Silver, Gold, Gems, Geodes | Bomb Skeletons | Upgrade route and loot farming |
| Darkstone Layer | Starts around Y -435 | Cobalt, Nocturnite, Black Ice | Darkstone Skeletons | Fast travel and deeper ore runs |
| Permafrost Layer | Starts around Y -723 | Chromium, Moonstone, Titanium, Wolframite | Ice Elemental, Darkstone Skeletons | High-tier farming and survival checks |
| Schwarzfrost Layer | Deep frozen endgame | Charged Ice, Eiskron, late-game loot | Boss-level pressure | Endgame materials and boss prep |

Structures matter as much as ores. Cave villages, ruins, towers, mineshafts, wormholes, and chest clusters can turn a normal run into a profitable one if you stop to inspect them safely.

| Structure | Best Use | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|
| Cave Village | Quests, loot, route planning | Low |
| Ruins | Chest checks and exploration | Medium |
| Tower | Vertical movement and card testing | Medium |
| Mineshaft | Ore access and deeper routing | Medium |
| Wormhole | Fast repositioning | High if used carelessly |

<Callout type="info" title="Depth Tip">
Do not rush straight down. Layer-by-layer play gives you better ore density, safer exits, and more chances to hit useful structures.
</Callout>
