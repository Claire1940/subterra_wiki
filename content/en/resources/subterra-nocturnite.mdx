### The Farming Cycle

```
[Overworld Lobby] ---> (Buy C4/TNT) ---> [Permafrost Portal] ---> [Locate Geodes/Ores]
       ^                                                                   |
       |                                                                   v
[Sell & Upgrade] <--- (Wormhole Potion) <--- [Mine with Explosives / Cards]
```

1. **Travel to Depth**: Use the **Permafrost Portal** (X 26, Y -754) to teleport directly to the deep cold layer.
2. **Locate Gem Geodes**: Seek out cave structures, ruins, and empty geode pockets. Nocturnite frequently spawns embedded inside these structures alongside other high-value gems like Sapphire and Diamond.
3. **Use Sticky Explosives**: Do not waste time manually swinging at tough blocks if they are clustered. Throw a **Small Sticky Bomb** or **Sticky C4** onto dense ore clusters to clear the surrounding terrain instantly and expose the hidden veins.
4. **Collect and Defend**: Keep your weapon ready. Deep mining noise will attract **Darkstone Skeletons** and **Ice Elementals**. Use your weapon's block/parry mechanic (`F` key) to mitigate damage.
5. **Safe Return**: Once your backpack is full, do not risk walking back up. Use a **Wormhole Potion** or **Return by Death Potion** to instantly teleport back to the Overworld lobby. Sell your haul at the Sell Area, smelt any raw ores, and repeat.

<Callout type="info" title="Sticky Explosive Recipes">
You can craft sticky explosives using **Slime Chunks** dropped by Slimes and Ice Slimes. 
- **Small Sticky Bomb**: 3x Slime Chunk + 1x Small Bomb
- **Sticky C4**: 3x Slime Chunk + 1x C4
</Callout>
