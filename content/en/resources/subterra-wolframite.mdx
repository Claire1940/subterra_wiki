<Section icon={Shield} title="Required Gear and Ability Card Loadouts">

Mining **subterra wolframite** is as much about survival as it is about digging. At depths below Y -700, the standard starter gear is entirely obsolete. You must focus on a balanced build that emphasizes mining power, movement speed, and durability. Ability Cards are the primary way to customize your character for these high-stakes environments.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pickaxe Power Check">
    Ensure your pickaxe is at least **Tier 14** (100 Power). Mining Wolframite with lower power results in excessive block-strike requirements, draining your oxygen before you can fill your backpack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Oxygen Management">
    Equip the **Extra Oxygen** Ability Card (Uncommon). Each card adds 25ml of oxygen, which is critical for the long travel times associated with the Permafrost layer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Economy Stacking">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Combat Readiness">

  </Step>
</Steps>
