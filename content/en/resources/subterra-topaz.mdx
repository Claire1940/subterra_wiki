<Section icon={Shield} title="What to Keep, Sell, and Save">

Your biggest mistake is overvaluing every drop the same way. Some materials are progress gates, while others are just temporary coin. Keep the materials that limit upgrades, and move the rest out of your bags before they slow the loop down.

<Callout type="info" title="Resource Priority">
Treat **topaz**, **amethyst shards**, and **cobalt** as route anchors, not random extras. If one of them is blocking your next upgrade, that material deserves priority.
</Callout>

| Material | Priority | Best Action |
|---|---|---|
| Topaz | High | Keep it for upgrades or gated crafts |
| Amethyst shards | Very high | Save every useful stack |
| Cobalt | High | Farm when a recipe or upgrade needs it |

| Extra bombs | Medium | Sell if they are not part of your build plan |
